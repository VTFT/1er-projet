How to invite Boe Tea

To get started, click on the blue and white button. It will take you to Discord's authorization page.

You'll have to login so Discord knows which servers are available for Boe Tea to join. Then choose the server you want Boe Tea in.

Next are the permissions (we advise you to keep everything checked or the bot won't work properly).

And... That's it. Congratulations and thank you for choosing Boe Tea !
